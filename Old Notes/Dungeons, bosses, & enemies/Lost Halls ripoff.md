Requires a knight to speedrun. Every enemy should be stunnable. Something like the escutcheon (lingering stun) should be highly effective here for something like the slime rooms (an immobile enemy that shoots a fuckton of bullets that slows down a run).

Readable procedural generation:
Always have 12 rooms before boss.
Always enter boss room from below.
Always enter secondary boss from below.
Boss room + boss entrance takes up 9 squares
Spawn room can only have multiple splits if it's near a corner. The split that heads into the corner must always be a dead end.
Boss room should always be on the opposite corner of spawn room.
